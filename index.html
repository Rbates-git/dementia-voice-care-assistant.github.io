CS 4850 Fall Senior Project

XZ-1 Dementia Care Voice Assistant

Overview
Our objective was to create a voice-assisted application designed to support informal caregivers of individuals with Alzheimer’s disease and related dementias (ADRD) through intuitive voice interactions. This application leveraged a fine-tuned Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)-based Language Model (LLM) to deliver immediate, contextually relevant responses to caregivers’ inquiries. It featured a user-friendly interface capable of processing voice commands and providing instant responses. The outcome was the development of an iOS application that offered immersive, engaging, and personalized non-pharmacological, evidence-based behavioral symptom intervention information. This enhanced the ability of informal dementia caregivers to manage the symptoms of their care recipients effectively.
